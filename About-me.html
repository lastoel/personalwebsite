About me

I am a statistics student at Utrecht University with a passion for education policy. Somehow, my work at The Netherlands Initiative for Education Research (NRO ) and my studies have found a common ground at Cito, where I am currently writing my thesis on causal inference with PISA data. I am particularly interested in research after features of education systems that aid the equality of educational opportunity.

I am based in The Hague in the Netherlands, but you'll find my heart in the Alps.
